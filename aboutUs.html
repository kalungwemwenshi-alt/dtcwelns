About Dotcom Wellness

Empowering Health Through Nature

Our Story

Dotcom Wellness was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between traditional African herbal wisdom and modern health science. Based in the vibrant city of Livingstone, Zambia, we stand at the crossroads of nature's bounty and scientific innovation.

Our journey began when a group of health enthusiasts and wellness advocates recognized the untapped potential of natural remedies in addressing modern health challenges. Today, we're proud to serve communities across Zambia and beyond with premium, scientifically-backed wellness products.

Our Mission

To provide world-class natural health products that empower individuals to take control of their wellness journey through the power of nature and science.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading wellness brand, recognized for innovation, quality, and our commitment to improving lives through sustainable health solutions.

Our Core Values

Quality First

We never compromise on product quality. Every item undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets international standards.

Natural Innovation

We blend traditional herbal knowledge with cutting-edge research to create effective, safe wellness solutions.

Community Focus

We believe in giving back and supporting local communities through education, employment, and sustainable practices.

Transparency

We maintain open communication about our ingredients, processes, and the science behind our products.
